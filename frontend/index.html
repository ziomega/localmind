How it works

Three steps. Zero effort on your part.

Local Mind runs silently in the background. You just browse normally — it does the rest.

01 —
👁
You browse, it watches

Every page you spend real time on — articles, docs, papers, recipes — gets quietly captured. Tabs you bounce off in two seconds? Ignored.

02 —
🧠
It understands, not just indexes

A tiny AI model runs on your device, turning each page into a semantic fingerprint. It understands meaning, not just keywords. "neural networks" matches "deep learning" too.

03 —
⚡
Ask in plain English

Search your memory the way you actually think. "that article about startup pricing" or "the recipe with tahini" — and it finds it, ranked by how relevant it actually is.
